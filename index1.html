Welcome to NetWIS Lab

The NetWIS lab is led by Dr. Wenye Wang,  an assistant professor in the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at North Carolina State University. The research pursued in the lab aims to advance the understanding of single- and multi-hop wireless networks and to design new algorithms and protocols in order to optimize networking performance. Currently, our research interests are focused on the following issues:

  • Mobile and secure computing.
  • Simulations and real-time performance evaluation of security mechanisms and routing protocols in wireless LANs and wireless Ad-Hoc networks.
  • Reliable transmission protocols in wireless sensor networks.
  • Mobility modeling and its impacts on networking characteristics of single-hop and multi-hop wireless networks.
  • Resiliency modeling and   control in multi-hop wireless networks and wireless sensors networks.
